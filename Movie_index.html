The Best Movies According To Saniya

Top 3 Movies Of All Time

Ajab Prem Ki Gajab Kahani

Movie featuring Ranbir Kapoor and Katrina Kaif is one of the best rom-com of all time.

Yeh Jawani Hai Deewani

Movie featuring Ranbir Kapoor, Deepika Padukone, Aditya Roy Kapoor and Kalki Kochelin is again one of the best movie showcasing friendship between 3 friends and love story between a studious girl and a world wanderer.

Zindagi Na Milegi Doobra

Movie featuring Hritik Roshan, Katrina Kaif, Farhan Akhtar, Kalki Kochelin and Abhay Deol is also one of the best rom-com showing friendship and love.
